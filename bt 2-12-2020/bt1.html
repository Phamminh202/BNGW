A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briands,or berger de bride, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The Briands which is belied to have in Franco, has been bred for centuries too herd and to protect sheep.The breed was used by the Franco Army as sentries,messengers and to search for wounder because of it's fine sense of hearing.Briands were used in the First world War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the population of briands is slowly recovering.Charlemagne,Napoleon,Thomas Jefferson ans Laffayette all oned ownes briands.
