The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal desgin, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Gardent originated in England and history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as a reation to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estate gardens.

The earlies cottage gardens were more practical than modern descendant, with an emphasis on vegetable and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
