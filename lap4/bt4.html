From Garden to Plate

A potatoger is a French term for an ornamenral vegetable or kitchen garden. Ofter flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty.The goal to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as their color and form.Many are trained to grow upward.A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herds for the home with very little maintenance.Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
